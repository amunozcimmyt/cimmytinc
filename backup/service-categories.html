Molecular Analyses

TEST: In today’s data-driven world, molecular analysis is revolutionizing the way we understand biology, agriculture, and biodiversity. Our advanced genotyping service brings together the strengths of DArTseq, DArTLD, DArTag, and KASP technologies to deliver high-resolution, cost-effective, and scalable solutions tailored to your research and breeding goals.

DArTseq offers a powerful platform that captures thousands of genome-wide SNPs and presence/absence markers, even in species without a reference genome. It’s an ideal choice for exploring genetic diversity, conducting genome-wide association studies, and implementing genomic selection strategies.

For projects requiring streamlined, cost-efficient genotyping: DArTLD provides a low-density alternative that maintains quality while optimizing for scale, perfect for large breeding programs. When precision is key, DArTag enables targeted genotyping by combining sequencing power with marker specificity. This makes it a valuable tool for marker-assisted selection and trait validation.

Complementing these approaches, KASP genotyping delivers rapid, accurate results for known SNPs, making it ideal for diagnostics, quality control, and the validation of key genetic traits.

These genotyping technologies open the door to a wide range of applications. From assessing genetic diversity and population structure to identifying trait-linked markers through Genome-Wide Association Studies (GWAS), our service supports the full spectrum of molecular breeding and conservation research. You can predict breeding values through genomic selection, trace pedigrees and confirm parentage, or ensure varietal purity and monitor introgression in hybrid lines.

Whether you're working with crops, forestry, or conservation species, our molecular analysis platform empowers you to make confident, data-driven decisions. By integrating these cutting-edge tools, we help you transform complexity into clarity, accelerating your path from insight to impact.
